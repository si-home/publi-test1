LP 1

Ich hab' mein Herz in Heidelberg verloren

 

by Raymond
 
Topics 78rpm, Popular Music
Publisher Derby
Digitizing sponsor Kahle/Austin Foundation
Contributor Internet Archive
Language German
 

Writer: Raymond

Lied.

Digitized at 78 revolutions per minute. Four stylii were used to transfer this record. They are 3.8mm truncated eliptical, 2.3mm truncated conical, 2.8mm truncated conical, 3.3mm truncated eliptical. These were recorded flat and then also equalized with Turnover: 375.0, Rolloff: -12.0.

The preferred versions suggested by an audio engineer at George Blood, L.P. have been copied to have the more friendly filenames.

Matrix number: 581B
Catalog number: V-581b
